# Shield of Faith
**School:** Abjuration | **Casting Time:** Bonus Action | **Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a prayer scroll) | **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes
**Concentration:** Yes | **Ritual:** No
**Classes:** Cleric, Paladin

A shimmering field surrounds a creature of your choice within range, granting it a +2 bonus to AC for the duration.
