> All information sourced from the Player's Handbook (2024) via dnd2024.wikidot.com.

## How Backgrounds Work

Each background provides the following:
- **Ability Score Increases** — Three ability scores are listed. You choose **one** of two options:
  - Increase **one score by 2** and **a different score by 1**, or
  - Increase **all three scores by 1**
  - No increase can raise a score above 20.
- **Two Skill Proficiencies**
- **One Tool Proficiency**
- **One Origin Feat** (granted at character creation)
- **Starting Equipment** (choose Option A for specific gear, or Option B for 50 GP)
