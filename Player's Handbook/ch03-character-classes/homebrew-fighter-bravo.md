### BRAVO

*Homebrew Fighter Subclass*

Wield Intellect as a Weapon

Bravos are fighters who treat combat as a puzzle to be solved rather than a test of sheer physical dominance. While many hail from the demimonde—the fringes of society where mercenaries, card sharps, and rogues mingle—others might be disgraced aristocrats, military tacticians, or self-taught survivors. They combine relentless pragmatism with keen observation, using their intellect to dissect an enemy's every weakness. A Bravo wins not by strength alone, but by knowing more about you than you know about yourself.

#### LEVEL 3: SCHOLAR OF THE DEMIMONDE

You have spent years among wanderers, gamblers, and confidence artists, picking up their skills and tongues. You gain proficiency in two of the following skills of your choice: Deception, Insight, Investigation, or Sleight of Hand. You also gain proficiency with one type of Gaming Set of your choice and learn one language of your choice.

In addition, you have Advantage on any check made to conceal a Light weapon or small object on your person.

#### LEVEL 3: PRAGMATIC ANALYSIS

You can use your keen intellect to dissect an enemy's fighting style. As a Bonus Action, you can Analyze one creature you can see within 60 feet of yourself. For 1 minute, or until the creature is reduced to 0 Hit Points, you gain the following benefit against it:

**Exploit Weakness.** When you hit the Analyzed creature with a weapon attack, you deal extra damage to it equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of +1).

If you Analyze a new creature, any previous Analyzed effect ends.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1). You regain all expended uses when you finish a Short Rest.

#### LEVEL 7: READ THE ROOM

Your ability to read people extends beyond the battlefield, and your sharp tactical instincts quicken your reactions.

**Keen Appraisal.** When you take the Study action to observe a creature, or when you spend at least 1 minute in conversation with a creature, you learn the following about it:

- Whether its Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores are each higher than, lower than, or equal to yours.
- Whether it is currently under any magical effect.
- Whether it is disguised or passing itself off as something it is not (though not necessarily what the disguise conceals).

**Tactical Awareness.** When you roll Initiative, you add your Intelligence modifier to the roll.

Additionally, your analysis of combat opponents deepens. While a creature is Analyzed by your Pragmatic Analysis, you add your Intelligence modifier (minimum of +1) to any saving throw that creature forces you to make.

#### LEVEL 10: THE MASK

Your carefully cultivated emotional armor makes your mind difficult to breach, and your years among gamblers and confidence artists have made you impossible to read.

**Sardonic Mind.** You gain proficiency in Wisdom saving throws. If you already have this proficiency, you instead gain proficiency in Intelligence or Charisma saving throws (your choice).

**Inscrutable.** Choose two skills in which you have proficiency. Your Proficiency Bonus is doubled for any ability check you make using those skills. Additionally, you can't be compelled to speak truthfully by magic, and no spell or effect can determine whether you are lying.

#### LEVEL 15: FEIGNED VULNERABILITY

You can control the pace of a fight while appearing to lose, luring an opponent into a devastating overextension.

When a creature you can see within range hits you with an attack, you can use your Reaction to make one weapon attack against the attacker. You add your Intelligence modifier to this attack roll in addition to any other modifiers. Whether or not your counterattack hits, you have Advantage on attack rolls you make against that creature until the end of your next turn.

#### LEVEL 18: CHOREOGRAPHED VIOLENCE

Your Pragmatic Analysis reaches a state of lethal perfection. While a creature is Analyzed by your Pragmatic Analysis, you predict their every movement:

**Perfect Read.** The Analyzed creature has Disadvantage on all attack rolls it makes against you.

**Flawless Anticipation.** The first attack roll you make against the Analyzed creature on each of your turns has Advantage.

Additionally, you regain one expended use of Pragmatic Analysis whenever you roll Initiative, provided you have no uses remaining.
