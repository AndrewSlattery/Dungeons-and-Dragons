### BRAVO

Dismantle Foes with Cunning and Observation

Bravos are cunning combatants who treat battle as a lethal game of psychological warfare. Where other fighters rely on overwhelming force or martial discipline, a Bravo systematically dismantles their opponents through ruthless observation. By cataloguing tells, manufacturing openings, and exploiting vulnerabilities with cold precision, a Bravo ensures the fight is decided long before the final blow is struck.

#### LEVEL 3: UNDERWORLD ADEPT

You gain proficiency in the Deception, Investigation or Stealth skill. You gain proficiency with one type of Gaming Set and learn two languages.

Hidden Arsenal. You have Advantage on any ability check made to conceal a Light weapon or Tiny object on your person.

#### LEVEL 3: TACTICAL ANALYSIS

As a Bonus Action, you can Analyze one creature you can see within 60 feet of yourself. Until the creature is reduced to 0 Hit Points or you finish a Long Rest, you gain the following benefits:

Shrewd Strike. When you take the Attack action, the first time you hit the Analyzed creature with an attack roll, you deal extra damage equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1). The extra damage is of the same type dealt by the attack. 

Unnerving Scrutiny. Whenever you make an Insight, Intimidation, or Deception check directed at an Analyzed creature, you can make it as an Intelligence check.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1). You regain all expended uses when you finish a Short or Long Rest.

#### LEVEL 7: COMBAT DEDUCTION

Your instincts and deductive reasoning have sharpened, granting you the following benefits:

Prepared. When you roll Initiative, you add your Intelligence modifier to the roll.

Thinking Ahead. When you make a saving throw against an effect caused by an Analyzed creature, you add your Intelligence modifier to the roll (minimum of 1).

Know Your Enemy. When you Analyze a creature, you can learn whether its Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores are higher, lower, or equal to yours, and whether it is under any magical effects or disguises.

#### LEVEL 10: IRON MASK

Your true intentions have become impossible to read, granting you the following benefits:

Inscrutable. You gain Expertise in one skill proficiency of your choice. Additionally, no spell or magical effect can force you to speak truthfully or detect whether you are lying.

Feigned Misstep. Once on each of your turns, when you miss with an attack roll against an Analyzed creature, you can add your Intelligence modifier to the attack roll, potentially turning the miss into a hit.

#### LEVEL 15: PUNISH RECKLESSNESS

When a creature you can see hits you with an attack roll, you can use your Reaction to make one weapon attack against the attacker, provided the attacker is within your weapon's reach or range. If your counterattack hits, you have Advantage on all attack rolls you make against that creature until the end of your next turn.

#### LEVEL 18: GRAND DESIGN

Your ability to manipulate the flow of battle reaches its pinnacle, granting you the following benefits:

Already There. When you roll Initiative, you can replace your Initiative total with your Intelligence score, gaining no benefit from any modifiers or other bonuses to the roll.

Perfect Read. Any creature currently Analyzed by you has Disadvantage on all attack rolls it makes against you.

Sweeping Assessment. When you use a Bonus Action to Analyze one or more creatures using your Cold Read feature, you can Analyze up to a number of creatures equal to your Intelligence modifier simultaneously, expending only one use of the feature. Additionally, when you roll Initiative and have no uses of this feature remaining, you regain one use.
