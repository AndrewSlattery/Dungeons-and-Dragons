### WORD DOMAIN

Speak Truth and Make All Listen

The Word Domain treats language as a divine force that shapes reality. Clerics who wield this magic are living heralds whose voices dictate the flow of battle, inspiring heroic hope in their allies while crushing enemies under absolute, undeniable commands.

Gods of this domain are patrons of truth, law, prophecy, and inspiration. They include strict arbiters of justice and divine messengers who deliver revelations to mortals. Their power is sought by diplomats, leaders, and peacekeepers who rely on unyielding mandates to forge alliances or uncover grand deceptions.

#### WORD DOMAIN SPELLS

When you reach a Cleric level specified in the Word Domain Spells table, you thereafter always have the listed spells prepared.

| Cleric Level | Prepared Spells                             |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| 3            | Command, Heroism, Suggestion, Zone of Truth |
| 5            | Sending, Tongues                            |
| 7            | Compulsion, Divination                      |
| 9            | Geas, Legend Lore                           |

#### LEVEL 3: HOPE AND HAVOC

As a Bonus Action, you present your Holy Symbol and expend a use of your Channel Divinity to make promises of support and discord. Choose a number of creatures you can see within 60 feet of yourself, up to a number equal to your Proficiency Bonus. For each chosen creature, choose one of the following effects:

- **Hope.** The creature has Advantage on the next D20 Test it makes before the end of your next turn.
- **Havoc.** The creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or have Disadvantage on the next D20 Test it makes before the end of your next turn.

#### LEVEL 3: INSPIRED PREACHER

Unfettered Tongue. You cannot be Deafened, and no effect can silence you.

Divine Emissary. As a Magic action, you touch a willing creature other than yourself and mark it as your deity's emissary. Until you finish a Short or Long Rest, the creature can speak and understand any languages you know, and it adds your Wisdom modifier to any Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Intimidation) check it makes. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.

#### LEVEL 6: BLESSED HERALD

While your Divine Emissary is active on a creature:

- You and that creature can communicate telepathically with each other while you are on the same plane of existence, regardless of distance.
- When that creature takes damage, you can take a Reaction to speak a warding word and reduce the damage taken by 1d8 + your Wisdom modifier.

#### LEVEL 17: WORD PERFECT

As a Bonus Action, you become an avatar of your deity's voice for 1 minute or until you have the Incapacitated condition. While in this state:

- **Echoes of Hope and Havoc.** Whenever you cast a Cleric spell of 1st level or higher with a Verbal component, you can immediately apply either the Hope or Havoc effect to one creature of your choice within 60 feet of you.
- **With One Voice.** You can treat your Divine Emissary target as the point of origin for any Cleric spell you cast that has a Verbal component.

Once you use the transformation, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest, unless you expend a spell slot of 5th level or higher to restore your use of it.
