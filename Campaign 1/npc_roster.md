# NPC Roster

## Brindlemark

### Sergeant Maren Thull
- **Role:** Constabulary sergeant, runs the Gatehouse
- **Appearance:** Stocky woman in her 40s, close-cropped grey hair, leather jerkin, ink-stained hands
- **Personality:** Briskly efficient, dry humour, treats mercenaries and contract workers with professional detachment — neither warmth nor hostility
- **Voice:** Short sentences. Doesn't waste words.

### Dalla Fenwick
- **Role:** Proprietor of the Kettle & Crow
- **Appearance:** Tall, broad-shouldered, ruddy-faced, early 50s. Apron perpetually stained.
- **Personality:** Genial and shrewd. Knows everyone's business. Prices are fair but firm.
- **Voice:** Warm, measured, with occasional pointed observations.

## Companions (Session 1+)

### Tessa Vael
- **Role:** Fellow hired guard; Wood Elf Ranger 1
- **Appearance:** Lean, angular, weather-tanned. Dark hair pulled back tight. Eyes that don't miss much. Wears well-used leather armour and carries a longbow that's clearly her primary relationship.
- **Personality:** Quiet, watchful, economical with words. Not unfriendly — just self-contained. Dry humour surfaces occasionally.
- **Voice:** Short, considered sentences. Slight pauses before speaking, as if deciding whether it's worth the effort.
- **Sheet:** `tessa_vael_sheet.md`

### Harlen Cade
- **Role:** Fellow hired guard; Human Rogue 1
- **Appearance:** Lean, medium height, dark curly hair, quick brown eyes. Clothes are road-worn but were once fine. Carries a rapier like someone who learned in a salle, not a gutter.
- **Personality:** Quick smile, easy charm, the alertness of a man who checks exits. Claims various pasts depending on the audience.
- **Voice:** Warm, slightly performative. Uses humour as deflection. Talks more when nervous.
- **Sheet:** `harlen_cade_sheet.md`

## Session 1 NPCs

### Pell Wender
- **Role:** Courier (the escort target)
- **Appearance:** Thin, nervous man in his 30s. Road cloak over clerk's clothes. Leather satchel chained to his left wrist.
- **Personality:** Fidgety, polite, clearly unused to travel. Grateful for the guards but anxious about everything.
- **Voice:** Slightly too fast, slightly too loud. Asks unnecessary questions.

### Gareth
- **Role:** Fourth hired guard (generic guard stat block)
- **Appearance:** Big, quiet man. Older — late 40s. Scarred hands. Carries a spear and wears a studded leather jerkin that's seen better years.
- **Personality:** Laconic. Does the job. Doesn't volunteer information about himself.
- **Voice:** Grunts, nods, occasional one-word answers. When he does speak in full sentences, people listen.
