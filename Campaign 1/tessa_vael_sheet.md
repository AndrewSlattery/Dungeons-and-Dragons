## Personality & Background

**Backstory:** Tessa grew up in a small wood elf settlement in the deep forest south of the Fenway. She left — or was asked to leave — under circumstances she doesn't discuss. She's been moving from town to town, taking tracking work, escort contracts, anything that keeps her outdoors and moving. The guard job heading toward Brindlemark suited her because it was heading the direction she was already going.

**Personality:** Quiet, watchful, economical with words. Observant to the point of making people uncomfortable — she notices things and remembers them. Not unfriendly, exactly — just self-contained. When she does speak, it tends to be pointed and practical. Has a dry, understated sense of humour that surfaces occasionally and catches people off guard.

**Fighting Style (narrative):** Archer first, always. Prefers to fight from distance and elevation. Carries two shortswords for close work but clearly considers melee an unwelcome development. Moves quietly and instinctively uses cover.
