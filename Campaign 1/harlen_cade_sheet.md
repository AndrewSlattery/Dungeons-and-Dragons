### Weapon Mastery Notes
- **Vex (Rapier):** On hit, you have advantage on your next attack roll against that creature before the end of your next turn.
- **Slow (Shortbow):** On hit, target's speed is reduced by 10 ft until start of your next turn.
- **Nick (Dagger):** When you make the extra attack of the Light property, you can make it as part of the Attack action instead of a Bonus Action. (Allows dagger as off-hand attack without using Bonus Action.)

## Personality & Background

**Backstory:** Harlen claims various backgrounds depending on the audience — merchant's son, disgraced clerk, reformed pickpocket, minor noble fallen on hard times. The truth is probably none of these, or all of them. What's clear is that he's educated, quick with his hands, and accustomed to leaving places faster than he arrived. He took the Brindlemark escort contract because he needed to leave somewhere quickly and the courier was leaving that morning.

**Personality:** Quick smile, quicker hands. Projects easy confidence and mild self-deprecation. Observant in a social way — notices people's reactions, their tells, what they want to hear. Can be genuinely charming when he chooses. There's something slightly nervous underneath it all, though — the alertness of a man who checks exits when he enters a room.

**Fighting Style (narrative):** Prefers not to fight at all, frankly. When forced, he's fast and dirty — rapier for reach, dagger for close quarters, and a strong instinct for positioning behind someone bigger (Sneak Attack). Not brave, exactly, but practical about violence when it finds him.
