- **Sap (Longsword):** On hit, target has disadvantage on its next attack roll before the start of your next turn.

## Personality & Background

**Backstory:** Rixon was apprenticed as a youth to a mildly arcane blacksmith — long enough to pick up a few cantrips and a knack for the theoretical side of magic, but the arrangement fell through for reasons he doesn't elaborate on. After that he joined the militia of a small city, where he learned the halberd and the shield wall. Now discharged, he takes guard contracts, escort jobs, and the occasional group venture — whatever pays.

**Personality:** Practical but imaginative. Thinks of himself as a denizen of the demimonde, where morals are fickle and loyalties more so, and true friends are rare and precious as diamonds. Projects the image of a tough henchman — and gleefully revels in inhabiting that character. Capable of rakish heroics or theatrical villainy as the mood takes him.

**Fighting Style (narrative):** Sword and shield by preference and practicality. Knows how to use halberds from his militia days, but does not carry one. Keeps a handaxe on his belt for close work or throwing. Not fast, not graceful — but strong, smart, and hard to put down.

## Visual Description

At 28 years old, Rixon is a physically imposing human, built broad and sturdy rather than sleek or graceful. Reflecting his lack of agility and immense physical power, he moves with a heavy, deliberate tread. His hands are thick and heavily calloused from his youth spent at a blacksmith's forge and his years holding a shield wall in the city militia. 

He dresses practically for his line of work, wearing a sturdy set of traveler's clothes beneath a heavy suit of chain mail. A small wooden shield rests on his back, complementing the longsword hanging at his hip. His belt is strictly utilitarian, holding two handaxes for close-quarters work alongside an unobtrusive leather case containing his gaming pieces. 

His dark hair is trimmed rather short on the sides with loose curls up on top, framing a face that naturally settles into the gruff, unbothered expression of a career tough guy—a look he gleefully cultivates. However, behind that theatrical henchman exterior, his eyes are sharp, betraying the keen intellect and practical imagination that keep him alive in the demimonde.
